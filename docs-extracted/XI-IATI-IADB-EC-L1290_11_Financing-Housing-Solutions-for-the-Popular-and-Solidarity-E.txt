               DOCUMENT OF THE INTER-AMERICAN DEVELOPMENT BANK




                                    RESOLUTION DE-100/24



   Ecuador. Loan 5910/OC-EC to Corporación Nacional de Finanzas Populares y Solidarias
          (CONAFIPS). Financing Housing Solutions for the Popular and Solidarity
            Economy. First Individual Operation under the Conditional Credit Line
                        for Investment Projects (CCLIP) EC-O0014



       The Board of Executive Directors

RESOLVES:

        That the President of the Bank, or such representative as he shall designate, is authorized,
in the name and on behalf of the Bank, to enter into such contract or contracts as may be
necessary with Corporación Nacional de Finanzas Populares y Solidarias (CONAFIPS), as
borrower, and with the Republic of Ecuador, as guarantor, for the purpose of granting the former
a financing aimed at cooperating in the execution of the project “Financing Housing Solutions for
the Popular and Solidarity Economy”, which constitutes the first individual operation under the
Conditional Credit Line for Investment Projects (CCLIP) EC-O0014, approved by Resolution
DE-99/24 on 7 August 2024. Such financing will be for the amount of up to US$70.000,000 from
the resources of the Bank’s Ordinary Capital, and will be subject to the Financial Terms and
Conditions and the Special Contractual Conditions of the Project Summary of the Loan Proposal.



                                   (Adopted on 7 August 2024)
