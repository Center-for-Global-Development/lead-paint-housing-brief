IV.      Execution

4.01     The Ministry of Finance, Economic Development, and Investment (MFEDI) w ill be the
         Executing Agency and will be responsible for the Program. The Central Executing Unit
         (CEU), a unit in the Ministry of Economic Development (MED), which is part of the
         MFEDI, w ill be the implementation unit. As the implementation unit, the CEU w ill serve
         as the primary point of contact for stakeholders and will participate in the coordination
         with all the relevant line ministries, municipalities, and additional stakeholders as utilities.
         The fo llowing staff will be hired or selected for the administration of the Program: (i) a
         program manager; (ii) a procurement officer; (iii) a finance officer; (iv) an environmental
         and social specialist; and (vi) a project engineer.

4.02     The MFEDI wi ll establish a Project Steering Committee (PSC) to provide strategic
         oversight to the Project. The PSC will be comprised of high-level representatives ofat least
         the follow ing ministries: the Ministry of Finance. the Ministry of Economic Development,
         and the M inistry of Local Government.
