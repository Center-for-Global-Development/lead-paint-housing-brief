               DOCUMENT OF THE INTER-AMERICAN DEVELOPMENT BANK




                                    RESOLUTION DE-148/19



                   Panama. Loan 4944/OC-PN to the Republic of Panama
        Program of Comprehensive Urban Development of Cities with Tourism Potential



       The Board of Executive Directors

RESOLVES:

       That the President of the Bank, or such representative as he shall designate, is authorized,
in the name and on behalf of the Bank, to enter into such contract or contracts as may be
necessary with the Republic of Panama, as borrower, for the purpose of granting it a financing to
cooperate in the execution of a “ Program of Comprehensive Urban Development of Cities with
Tourism Potential”. Such financing will be for the amount of up to US$100,000,000, from the
resources of the Bank’s Ordinary Capital, and will be subject to the Financial Terms and
Conditions and the Special Contractual Conditions of the Project Summary of the Loan Proposal.



                                (Adopted on 11 December 2019)
