                              URBAN UPGRADING & REVITALIZATION PROGRAMME
                                               GENERAL PROCUREMENT NOTICE

    Institution: Inter-American Development Bank
    Country: Republic of Trinidad and Tobago
    Project: Urban Upgrading and Revitalization Programme
    Sector: Housing and Urban Development
    Abstract: The Programme will include the procurement of residential civil works relating to
    infrastructure development works for the upgrading of existing water reticulation network, road
    pavement structure & layout and storm water drainage plan; building refurbishment, site and
    streetscaping infrastructure, and landscaping to increase the attractiveness, resilience, and
    sustainable use of urban public spaces and facilities; consulting services for various sector studies,

    Loan/Credit/Grant No.: 5048/OC-TT (TT-L1056) and 5049/OC-TT (TT-L1057)

    The Government of the Republic of Trinidad and Tobago has received financing in the amount
    equivalent to US 50 million from the Inter-American Development Bank (IDB), and it intends to apply part
    of the proceeds to payments for goods, works, related services and consulting services to be procured
    under the project Urban Upgrading and Revitalization Programme. Bidding will be governed by the Inter-
    American Development Bank’s eligibility rules and procedures.

    The programme is financed through an investment loan with two modalities: TT-L1056, a Multiple Works
    Programme (MWP) that finances urban residential infrastructure and urban regeneration of public
    spaces; and TT-L1057, a Specific Investment Loan (SIL) which finances affordable housing subsidies
    and strengthening of housing and urban development sector stakeholders’ capacities. The general
    objective of the programme is to contribute to the improvement of the quality of the urban built
    environment. The specific objectives are to: (i) improve the habitability in urban settlements on State-
    owned lands; (ii) improve the physical quality and economic performance of urban public spaces; (iii)
    enhance housing conditions for low income households; and (iv) strengthen the capacity of supply side
    stakeholders to satisfy effective housing demand.
    The works, goods and consulting services to be contracted include the following: a) renovation and
    upgrade of Eastside Plaza; upgrade of the Scarborough Esplanade: b) construction of urban residential
    infrastructure; c) procurement of GIS and relevant software and office furniture; and d) procurement of
    consultancy services for various sector studies, including a national housing market study; a drainage
    and waste-water management Plan; a Study of appropriate development standards in dense, hillside,
    urban communities; and for Tobago, an Infrastructure Investment Plan to guide sustainable
    development.
    Procurement processes of contracts financed by the Inter-American Development Bank will be
