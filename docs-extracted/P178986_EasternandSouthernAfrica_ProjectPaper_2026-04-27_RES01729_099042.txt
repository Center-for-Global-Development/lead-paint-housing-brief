  I. PROJECT STATUS AND RATIONALE FOR RESTRUCTURING

      A. Project Status
The Africa Green Resilient and Inclusive Housing De-Risking (GRIHD) Facility project was approved on April 1, 2025. The project
effectiveness conditions were fulfilled on December 5, 2025. These included: (i) signing the Subsidiary Agreement, (ii) finalizing
and adopting the Project Operations Manual (POM), (iii) executing the financing agreement and fulfilling its prerequisites, and
(iv) finalizing the Inclusion Strategy. The documentation evidencing the achievement of the first performance-based condition
(PBC1) [Green Housing Value Chain (GHVC) Fund legally established] has been provided by TDB. TDB will receive an advance of
US$10 million, which will be used to capitalize the Green Housing Value Chain Fund. Upon confirmation that the Fund has been
capitalized, the corresponding expenditure will be recorded for purposes of PBC verification. TDB reaffirmed its commitment to
meeting the other PBCs, sustaining implementation progress, and delivering demonstrable results.


  II. DESCRIPTION OF PROPOSED CHANGES

This proposed restructuring would extend the closing date of the recipient-executed grant (TF-C7301) of US$5 million
associated with the project from June 30, 2026, to June 30,2028.
