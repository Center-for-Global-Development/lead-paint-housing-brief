The Government of the Republic of Trinidad and Tobago has received a loan from the Inter-American
Development Bank (IDB) toward the cost of the
Urban Upgrading and Revitalization Programme, and it intends to apply part of the proceeds of this loan to
payments under the contract for Refurbishment
of the Eastside Plaza – P2304.
The National Insurance Property Development Company Limited (NIPDEC) now invites sealed bids from
eligible bidders for the Refurbishment of Eastside
Plaza. Eastside Plaza is located in the City of Port of Spain, on the site of the former Eastern Market. The mall
spans from Charlotte Street on its western
entrance to George Street on the eastern entrance.
The works involve demolition, building works, roof works, glazing works, supply, and installation of – finishes,
lifts and escalators, sanitary appliances and
fittings, furniture and equipment, HVAC equipment, water storage and potable water pumping system, fire
suppression system and electrical infrastructure.
The delivery/construction period is 520 calendar days following the Commencement Date.
This Request for Bids (RFB) will be conducted through the international competitive bidding procedures
specified in the Inter-American Development Bank’s
Policies for The Procurement of Works and Goods Financed by The Inter-American Development Bank and is
open to bidders from all countries as defined in
the guidelines.
