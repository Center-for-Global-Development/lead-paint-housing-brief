              DOCUMENT OF THE INTER-AMERICAN DEVELOPMENT BANK




                                  RESOLUTION DE-38/22



 Argentina. Loan 5557/OC-AR to the Argentine Republic. Barrio Improvement Program. Fourth
    Individual Operation Under the Conditional Credit Line for Investment Projects (CCLIP)
                  Established by Agreement on 29 March 2007 (AR-X1004)



       The Board of Executive Directors

RESOLVES:

        That the President of the Bank, or such representative as he shall designate, is
authorized, in the name and on behalf of the Bank, to enter into such contract or contracts as
may be necessary with the Argentine Republic, as borrower, for the purpose of granting it a
financing to cooperate in the execution of the Barrio Improvement Program, which constitutes
the fourth individual operation under the Conditional Credit Line for Investment
Projects (CCLIP), established by Agreement on 29 March 2007 (AR-X1004), approved by
Resolution DE-5/07 on 31 January 2007. Such financing will be in the amount of up to
US$150,000,000, from the resources of the Bank’s Ordinary Capital, and will be subject to the
Financial Terms and Conditions and the Special Contractual Conditions of the Project Summary
of the Loan Proposal.



                                      (Adopted on 6 July 2022)
