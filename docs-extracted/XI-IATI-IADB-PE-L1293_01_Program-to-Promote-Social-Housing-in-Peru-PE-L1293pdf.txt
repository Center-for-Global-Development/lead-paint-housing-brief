               DOCUMENT OF THE INTER-AMERICAN DEVELOPMENT BANK




                                    RESOLUTION DE-172/24



                        Peru. Loan 5961/OC-PE to the Republic of Peru
                          Program to Promote Social Housing in Peru



       The Board of Executive Directors

RESOLVES:

        That the President of the Bank, or such representative as he shall designate, is authorized,
in the name and on behalf of the Bank, to enter into such contract or contracts as may be
necessary with the Republic of Peru, as borrower, for the purpose of granting it a financing to
cooperate in the execution of the Program to Promote Social Housing in Peru. Such financing will
be for the amount of up to US$300,000,000 from the resources of the Bank’s Ordinary Capital
and will be subject to the Financial Terms and Conditions and the Special Contractual Conditions
of the Project Summary of the Loan Proposal.



                                 (Adopted on 5 December 2024)
