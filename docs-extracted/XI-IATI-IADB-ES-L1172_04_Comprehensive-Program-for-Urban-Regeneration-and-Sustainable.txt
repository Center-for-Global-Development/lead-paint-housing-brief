               DOCUMENT OF THE INTER-AMERICAN DEVELOPMENT BANK




                                     RESOLUTION DE-83/25



                 El Salvador. Loan 6041/OC-ES to the Republic of El Salvador
                     Comprehensive Program for Urban Regeneration and
                       Sustainable Revitalization of Municipal Markets in
                                 Santa Tecla and La Libertad



       The Board of Executive Directors

RESOLVES:

        That the President of the Bank, or such representative as he shall designate, is authorized,
in the name and on behalf of the Bank, to enter into such contract or contracts as may be
necessary with the Republic of El Salvador, as borrower, for the purpose of granting it a financing
aimed at cooperating in the execution of the Comprehensive Program for Urban Regeneration
and Sustainable Revitalization of Municipal Markets in Santa Tecla and La Libertad. Such
financing will be for the amount of up to US$170,000,000, from the resources of the Bank’s
Ordinary Capital, and will be subject to the Financial Terms and Conditions and the Special
Contractual Conditions of the Project Summary of the Loan Proposal.



                                (Adopted on 17 September 2025)
