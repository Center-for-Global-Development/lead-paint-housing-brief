               DOCUMENT OF THE INTER-AMERICAN DEVELOPMENT BANK




                                     RESOLUTION DE-83/24



          Uruguay. Loan 5896/OC-UR to the Fideicomiso Integración Social y Urbana,
              through the Corporación Nacional Financiera – Administradora de
                  Fondos de Inversión S.A., in its capacity as trustee. Social
                         and Urban Integration Program in Uruguay



       The Board of Executive Directors

RESOLVES:

         That the President of the Bank, or such representative as he shall designate, is authorized,
in the name and on behalf of the Bank, to enter into such contract or contracts as may be necessary
with the Fideicomiso Integración Social y Urbana, through the Corporación Nacional Financiera –
Administradora de Fondos de Inversión S.A., in its capacity trustee, as borrower, and with the
Oriental Republic of Uruguay, as guarantor, for the purpose of granting the former a financing to
cooperate in the execution of the Social and Urban Integration Program in Uruguay. Such financing
will be for an amount of up to US$60.000.000 from the Ordinary Capital resources of the Bank, and
will be subject to the Financial Terms and Conditions and the Special Contractual Conditions of the
Project Summary of the Loan Proposal.



                                    (Adopted on 26 June 2024)
