The Government of Belize has received financing in the amount of USD 12,500,000 from the Inter-
American Development Bank (IDB) and intends to utilize the funds for eligible payments related to the
procurement of goods, works, related services, and consulting services under the "Sustainable and Inclusive
Urban Development" program. This project has received USD 10,000,000 in the form of a loan and the
additional USD 2,500,000 in the form of a grant.

The overarching goal is to contribute to the sustainable urban development of municipalities of northern
Belize. The specific objectives are: (i) to improve access to selected urban services; (ii) to support local
economic growth; and (iii) to facilitate the social and economic integration of vulnerable populations, such
as migrant population and women.

Procurement processes of contracts financed by the Inter-American Development Bank will be conducted in
accordance with the Policies for Procurement of Goods and Works financed by the Inter-American
Development Bank (GN-2349-15 – May 2019), and is open to all eligible bidders as defined in the policies.
For these procurement processes domestic preference shall be applied to compare bids. Consulting services
will be selected and contracted in accordance with the Policies for the Selection and Contracting of
Consultants financed by the Inter-American Development Bank (GN-2350-15 – May 2019).

Specific procurement notices will be published as they become available pursuant to the Procurement Plan
published in https://www.iadb.org/en/projects/project-procurement. For consulting services expected to
cost an amount equivalent to US $200,000.00 or more, a request for expressions of interest contracts will
be advertised in https://mof.gov.bz/ website and national newspapers of wide circulation within Belize.
